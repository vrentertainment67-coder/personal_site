## What Actually Affects Wedding DJ Pricing in Bangalore

Five factors determine the quote you'll receive:

**1. Hours of performance** — A 2-hour sangeet set and a 6-hour sangeet + reception combination are priced entirely differently. Always confirm exactly how many hours are included.

**2. Equipment quality** — A Pioneer CDJ-3000 + professional PA rig costs 4–5× more than a basic laptop + rental speaker setup. The difference is audible in the room.

**3. Experience tier** — A DJ with 2 years and a weekend gig history charges very differently from one with 15 years and an international portfolio. Experience is not decoration — it's what happens when the unexpected happens on the night.

**4. Travel distance** — Within Bangalore, travel is often included. Destination weddings in Goa, Udaipur, or international venues involve flights, accommodation, and equipment logistics — all of which affect the quote.

**5. Event type and complexity** — A sangeet with family performance cues, custom mashups, mic work, and a 300-person mixed crowd is a more complex job than a straightforward reception. Complexity is priced accordingly.

<SectionDivider label="04 — RED FLAGS" />

## Warning Signs When Hiring a Cheap DJ

<CalloutBox variant="warning" label="RED FLAG">
No contract offered. If they're reluctant to put it in writing, they're not a professional — they're a person with a speaker and a Spotify account.
</CalloutBox>

- **No contract** — non-negotiable minimum standard
- **Can't show real performance footage** — only highlight reels
- **Vague about equipment** — "I have a good system" is not an answer
- **No backup gear** — what happens if the primary rig fails at 10pm?
- **Cash only, no receipt** — no paper trail means no accountability
- **Unavailable for a pre-event call** — a professional always does a prep conversation
