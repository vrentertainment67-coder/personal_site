About

Active since 2007, DJ VIC brings nearly two decades of international experience across India, the UK, and Australia. His sound is a fusion of English and Indian music, delivered through original remixes and mashups crafted uniquely for every venue and audience.

Having collaborated with major Indian labels including Universal Music, VIC specialises in audio-visual compositions that move both the dancefloor and the digital space. Whether in clubs, corporate events, or curated concept nights — he designs experiences, not just playlists.

Signature

DJ VIC is known for his audio-visual sets — original music synced with visuals, crafted as a single, immersive performance. A format he has taken to clubs, corporate stages, and destination weddings across India and internationally.
