About the Show

Beyond the Music

Hosted by DJ VIC — nearly two decades in nightlife across 18 countries — The VIC Fix goes beyond the music to document the people, places, and moments that shaped India's after-dark culture. DJs, bartenders, venue founders, promoters, artists: the ones who built the scene, told in their own words.
